We are the data controller for personal information collected through our website and in the course of delivering our Services. For GDPR purposes, our representative can be reached at: legal@ocypheris.com

3. Information We Collect
3.1 Account and Contact Information
We collect information you provide when you engage with us, including:
	•	Name, job title, and company name
	•	Email address, phone number, and mailing address

To perform security assessments, we may be granted read-only access to your AWS account. This may involve exposure to:
	•	AWS resource configurations, IAM policies, security group rules, and service metadata
	•	Log data, audit trails, and compliance reports within your AWS environment

We access only what is necessary to perform the agreed assessment. We do not retain your AWS credentials or raw environment data beyond the engagement period.
3.3 Website Usage Data
When you visit our website, we may collect standard analytics data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral source, typically through cookies or similar technologies.

For clients in the European Union, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

	•	Legitimate interests: Analytics and service improvement, where such interests are not overridden by your rights.
	•	Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
	•	Consent: Where we have obtained your explicit consent (e.g., marketing communications), which you may withdraw at any time.

8. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. These include:
